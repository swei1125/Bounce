Welcome to Bounce!

How to play:

  • Click anywhere on the game board to start the game
  • Click on paddles to rotate them
  • Press space to release the ball
  • is the start position of the ball
  • is where the ball should go at the end
  • Paddles:
    • Blue paddle is regular paddle
    • Red paddle will disappear when the ball hits it
  • Click to the next level
  • Game over when the ball flies out of the board
  • Click to retry the same level
